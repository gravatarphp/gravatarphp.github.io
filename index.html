GravatarPHP Use and manage your Gravatar

About

There are thousands of Gravatar clients written in PHP. Some of them are good, actively maintained, but most of them simply useless. (See: Phil Sturgeon's PHP Wars post.) This initiative tries to collect the best ideas from all existing implementations to provide an easy to use, but a powerfull tool to integrate Gravatar into applications. Some new, never seen tools will also be introduced.

To package authors! You are more than welcome to contribute your projects to this initiative. This is not trying to be the de facto implementation provider.

Components

XML-RPC API

Connect to Gravatar through XML-RPC to manage your addresses and images. Under the hood lstrojny/fxmlrpc is used.

URL Builder (WIP)

Build URLs to your avatar and profile.
